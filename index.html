Who We Are

We are the Alpha Eta Chapter of Kappa Phi Lambda Sorority, Inc. located at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Kappa Phi Lambda is an Asian-interest sorority upholding our three pillars of sisterhood, service, and cultural diversity. With over 4,000 sisters all over the world, the Kappa sisterhood can be found anywhere you go.

Pillars

We distinguish ourselves on our three pillars: Sisterhood, Service, and Cultural Diversity. They are the foundation of our sorority and the virtues that bring us together in sisterhood. They allow us to become stronger women, provide service, and help advocate for cultural diversity within the greater community.
